# Kyber Crystal Ore

Kyber Crystal Ore can be mined on [Planet Ilum](../planets/planet-ilum.md).

<img width="256" height="256" alt="Kyber Crystal Ore" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cc2ba13a-83d7-4d36-8943-4337a510dd68" />

Deposits of the ore can be found in clusters of three in rarely appearing thermal vent outcrops scattered across the planet's surface.
