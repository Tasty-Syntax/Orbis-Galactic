# Durasteel Ingot

Durasteel is one of the most important crafting materials in Orbis Galactic.

<img width="99" height="101" alt="Durasteel Ingot" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f89e54e8-fbad-4281-89a2-7f5e141a92d1" />

## What It Is Used For

Durasteel is used in:

- Laser Weapon Crafter crafting
- Saber Forge crafting
- Other higher-value tech-style recipes

## How to Make It

Smelt it in a Furnace using:

- `3` Iron Bars
- `1` Charcoal
