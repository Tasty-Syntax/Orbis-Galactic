# Kyber Crystal Bleeding

To bleed a [Kyber Crystal](kyber-crystals.md), throw a crystal of any color other than **white** or **red** on the ground and select the **Force Lightning** [Force Power](../the-force/force-powers.md).

<img width="700" height="400" alt="Hytale2026-04-13_09-53-55" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a760fcbc-f42d-42f7-8fcd-9c72fc44ab1b" />

Then use your Force Lightning on the crystal.

<img width="700" height="400" alt="Hytale2026-04-13_09-55-25" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/23cb31f8-757b-4b9d-8f89-e0cacdbc897c" />

The crystal will be bled and forced into changing into a **red** [Kyber Crystal](kyber-crystals.md).

<img width="700" height="400" alt="Hytale2026-04-13_09-54-03" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1efc0acf-436e-48e5-9b02-ec49b9893a60" />

To make a **white** Kyber Crystal, simply shoot lightning at the **red** crystal once more.
