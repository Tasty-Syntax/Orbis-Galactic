# Planet Tatooine

![Tatooine](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c97a785b-f5be-4589-827d-0e6ecc3ea260)

Tatooine is a remote, arid desert world defined by its twin suns and relentless heat, where endless dune seas and rocky wastelands dominate the landscape. Once a more hospitable planet, prolonged exposure to intense solar radiation and shifting climatic conditions has stripped it of nearly all surface water, leaving behind a harsh environment where survival depends on ingenuity and endurance. Scattered settlements persist through moisture farming and trade, extracting water directly from the dry air to sustain life.
