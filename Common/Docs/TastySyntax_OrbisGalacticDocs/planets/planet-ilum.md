# Planet Ilum

<img width="360" height="370" alt="Ilum" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/304addc5-7057-4189-85b6-7c02b28b00cd" />

Ilum is a remote, ice-covered world best known for its deep connection to the Force and its exclusive reserves of [Kyber Crystals](../items/kyber-crystals.md). Beneath its frozen surface lies a volatile and geologically active core, where extreme heat and pressure give rise to rare volcanic phenomena within an otherwise glacial environment. These violent interactions between fire and ice are responsible for the formation of Kyber Crystal ore deposits, making Ilum one of the most valuable and dangerous planets in the galaxy.

<img width="2558" height="500" alt="Ilum1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/253b5fd5-6038-47e4-bacf-2fbbc97a6a51" />

Scarcely scattered over the surface of Ilum, there have been reports of [Ancient Jedi Temples](ancient-jedi-temple.md) which may hold the key to tapping into the [Force](../the-force/force-powers.md) yourself.
