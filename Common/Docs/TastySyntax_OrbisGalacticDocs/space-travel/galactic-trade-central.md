# Galactic Trade Central

Using your **Galactic Trade Terminal** located inside of your Galactic Trade Central gives you access to the entirety of the galactic market.

<img width="700" height="300" alt="Hytale2026-04-11_13-40-03" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/90ba6998-d4a9-4941-bd12-22822229b348" />

The market periodically experiences **Events** modifying prices, causing them to either skyrocket or plummet depending on which event is taking place. Each item responds directly to purchases — for example: if a player buys hundreds of stacks of dirt on the Galactic Market, when the next market update cycle happens the price of dirt will increase significantly to match demand.

The past price and current market value of all listed items can be viewed by pressing the **History** button. New items or old items are listed/relisted in the market with every market cycle.
