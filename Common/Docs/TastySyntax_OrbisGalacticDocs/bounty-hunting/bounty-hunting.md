# Bounty Hunting

Bounty hunting can be accessed through a [Bounty Board](bounty-board.md) located in [Mos Eisley](../planets/mos-eisley.md).

<img width="700" height="400" alt="Hytale2026-04-12_22-31-38" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cf207bbd-c922-4a31-9904-00ac21a5eb56" />

When accessing the Bounty Board, 3 different bounties will appear. Choosing one gives you a location on a planet and a target name.

<img width="700" height="400" alt="Hytale2026-04-12_22-31-44" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b66ed3fd-e0ac-4319-a951-0c99c955b5a9" />

Travel to the location, defeat the enemy and claim the bounty!
