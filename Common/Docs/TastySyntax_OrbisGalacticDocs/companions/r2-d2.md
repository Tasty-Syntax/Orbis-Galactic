# R2-D2

R2-D2 is a little companion who can follow you around while making silly noises.

<img width="300" height="250" alt="R2-D2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/21c0bd64-76de-4b05-beaa-20c4cca1e80e" />

## How to Get R2-D2

To obtain R2-D2, visit a [Scrap Trader](../vendors/scrap-trader.md) in a [Jawa Sandcrawler](../planets/jawa-sandcrawler.md) and purchase it for **50 Scrap**.

## Coloring Tool

The R2-D2 Coloring Tool is another Scrap Trader item. It can be purchased from a [Scrap Trader](../vendors/scrap-trader.md) for **47 Scrap**.

Use it if you want to:
- Personalize your droid by looping through some colors
