# Force Holocron

<img width="72" height="70" alt="Force Holocron" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8277ee14-ec0a-4eff-83d9-731f083d495c" />

The Force Holocron is used to select a [Force Power](force-powers.md). Use your **Secondary** action to open up a radial menu of Force Powers.

## Crafting

To craft the Force Holocron, you first need to acquire all required components:

- [Broken Force Holocron](broken-force-holocron.md)
- [Memory Crystal](../items/memory-crystal.md)
- [Holocron Core](../items/holocron-core.md)

Once those components have been collected, go to an [Ancient Jedi Temple](../planets/ancient-jedi-temple.md) on [Planet Ilum](../planets/planet-ilum.md). In the lower section there is a Trader who lets you build a Force Holocron.
