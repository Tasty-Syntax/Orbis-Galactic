# Force Powers

There are currently 4 active Force abilities you can learn and use.

Using Force abilities requires possession of a [Force Holocron](force-holocron.md) and acquiring training scrolls, found in random loot chests on [Planet Tatooine](../planets/planet-tatooine.md).

The Force Holocron can also be put in your off-hand and the currently active Force Power can be activated using **Ability 3** (Default: R).

<img width="310" height="114" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e960ee5d-bcf6-4295-b7f4-121d44961ae2" />

Using the Force Holocron opens a radial menu allowing you to choose a Force Power by pressing **Secondary** (Default: Right Mouse Button) with the item in hand.

<img width="700" height="400" alt="Hytale2026-04-11_13-58-30" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b8db4d94-a06b-4d8b-93f3-f930faf9f6fd" />

## Available Force Powers

### Force Jump
<img width="73" height="70" alt="Force Jump" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5d86df0d-5ba7-4242-b44a-f87e202b079c" />

Launches you forward using the Force.

### Force Pull
<img width="73" height="70" alt="Force Pull" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5b1903a6-66fa-400d-9587-37da35218960" />

Pulls your target towards you. Holding the ability down increases the amount of Force they are pulled with.

### Force Push
<img width="72" height="72" alt="Force Push" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/389b1b72-2dd7-445a-b8fc-121f9988b97c" />

Pushes your target away from you. Holding the ability down increases the amount of Force they are pushed with.

### Force Lightning
<img width="73" height="71" alt="Force Lightning" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ad983b53-f3b6-4c8a-a284-6bb6b095c436" />

Shoots Force Lightning from your hands, dealing damage to a target in front of you.

Force Lightning can also be applied to a [Kyber Crystal](../items/kyber-crystals.md) on the floor to start the process of [Kyber Crystal Bleeding](../items/kyber-crystal-bleeding.md).
