# Lightsaber

There are currently 7 colors of lightsabers in the mod with 3 different [Lightsaber Hilt](lightsaber-hilts.md) variations.

- Lightsaber Hilts can be assembled at the [Saber Hilt Bench](saber-hilt-bench.md)
- Full Lightsabers can be assembled at the [Saber Forge](saber-forge.md)
